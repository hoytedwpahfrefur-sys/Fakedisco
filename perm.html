Run a background console session
A lightweight process will run on your device to maintain your bot's connection to Discord in real time.
Send and read messages as your bot
Biscord will read incoming messages and send responses on behalf of your bot using your token.
Access server and channel data
View the servers your bot is in, their channels, members, and message history.
Keep your session alive in the background
The console will stay connected so your bot responds even when the tab is inactive.
